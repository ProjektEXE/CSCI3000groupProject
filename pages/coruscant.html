Coruscant

Overview

Welcome to Coruscant, the galaxy's political and cultural capital. This planet-wide metropolis boasts levels of history and luxury stretching from sky to core. Whether you're exploring Jedi Temple ruins or clubbing in the Underworld, Coruscant has it all.

Attractions

Upper City

Experience elite shopping, sky lounges, and Senate views high above the clouds.

The Underworld

Venture below to neon-lit speeder alleys, bounty cantinas, and spice markets.

Jedi Temple Ruins

Walk among the remnants of the Jedi Order's iconic temple and archives.

Culture

Coruscant is home to billions of beings from across the galaxy.

Expect diverse languages, fashion, and customs with a strong Republic legacy.

The mix of Core World nobility and street culture defines its unique atmosphere.

Local Cuisine

Corellian Ale

A beloved brew found in Coruscant cantinas, known for its kick and fizz.

Standard Ration Pack

Reimagined rations elevated to haute cuisine in upscale Coruscant restaurants.

Blue Milk Cocktail

Served chilled with spice extract and glowing ice cubes. Very popular in upper districts.

When to Visit?

Plan your visit during the Festival of Lights, when Coruscant glows from top to bottom in honor of unity and peace. Expect parades, levitating fireworks, and endless music.
